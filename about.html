About Us

Café Royal offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in town. We are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. We offer a delicious variety of coffee roasted in house made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages.
All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health.
You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker.
We look forward to serving you at Café Royal!
